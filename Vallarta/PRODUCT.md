# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Property Owner**: Uses the app to passively supervise operations, review top-level metrics, and ensure the property is performing well.
- **Property Manager**: Uses the app for active day-to-day operations and management.
- **Tenants**: Uses the app to book their stay and manage their reservations.

## Product Purpose
A luxury webapp to manage a high-end property in Puerto Vallarta. The primary goal is to provide seamless operational oversight for the owner through key metrics, while facilitating property management and tenant bookings in a single, premium ecosystem.

## Brand Personality
Luxury, calm, relaxing. 
The interface should evoke a high-end Puerto Vallarta experience. It must feel premium, deliberate, and sophisticated, avoiding any cluttered or purely utilitarian aesthetics often found in standard management software.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dense, utilitarian dashboard software (e.g., standard property management tools).
- Overly flashy, neon interfaces, or anything that feels rushed or cheap.

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Calm**: The interface should breathe. Use ample white space and deliberate typography to create a relaxed, luxury feel.
- **Effortless Oversight**: For the owner, metrics should be immediately clear and beautifully presented, requiring zero effort to interpret.
- **Premium Restraint**: Avoid unnecessary borders, harsh shadows, or dense grids. Every element must feel intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No specific requirements beyond standard web best practices.
